Description

GH-ERP is a custom-built enterprise resource planning solution developed for GH, a Kuwait-based company offering engineering and construction services to clients such as Construction, Design, Eng. Hakeem (AIU), and Eng. Hakeem (Bidaa). The system was tailored to streamline internal operations and client service delivery. I developed a centralized Admin Panel using Laravel 11 and SQL, allowing the admin to manage clients, create service-based projects, and assign services accordingly. The system also includes:
. Quotation & Invoice Generation
. Client & Project Management
. HR Management
. Equity & Asset Management
. Petty Cash & Expense Tracking (for both projects and the company)
. Financial Reports including Balance Sheet and Profit & Loss
. Dashboard
. Fund Tracking

The system is integrated with seven different bank modules for fund calculations:
. Construction Bank
. Design Bank
. Eng. Hakeem (AIU) Bank
. Eng. Hakeem (Bidaa) Bank
. Indemnity & Leave Bank
. Petty Cash Holder
This project plays a critical role in managing financial data, generating exportable reports, and enabling transparency for business decisions.
